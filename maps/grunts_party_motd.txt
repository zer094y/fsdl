Welcome to 'Grunts Party'; a collection of 20 mini-games that will keep you challenged! Inspired by custom party mods from Warcraft 3, this game offers a diverse range of challenges that engage players of every skill level. The game is best played with 2 players or more. The map provides approximately 35+ minutes of gameplay for 2 players, with extended gameplay available for additional players.
