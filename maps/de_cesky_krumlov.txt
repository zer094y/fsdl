History:

Cesky Krumlov, Winter 1943

Czechoslovakia is occupied by the Axis Armies, but a group of Czech liberation will try to make a key attack in the beautiful town of Cesky Krumlov.

The Czech Resistance Member have capture the Axis HQ and build some defence like Ammunition Garage, Captured Tiger Tank and AA Gun.

Axis must destroy their defence and defeat the Resistance.
The Resistance member must defend and eliminate all of Axis Forces.
