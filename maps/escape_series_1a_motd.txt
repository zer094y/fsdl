Story
-----
You are a group of normal people who have been
kidnapped by a group of scientists in an under
ground lab, they have abused you, mistreated 
you and messed around with your DNA. Now once 
they were done with you they locked you in a 
cell at the bottom of the lab complex. But 
now something has happened and everyone is 
dead, all but you locked away in the cell 
however… Now it’s up to you and your team 
mates to try and ESCAPE!
